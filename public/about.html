Basketball

I started playing basketball when I was in primary school. But at that time I didn't put effort in playing it well so it's just a way for me to pass the leisure time and playing with my friends.

However, as I fell in love with watching NBA basketball and loved the players in it, I began playing basketball more skillfully and competitively.

Now, I love the sport so much and am willing to spend a lot of time working on my shooting and dribbling skills.

Football

I also started playing football when I was in primary school. At that time I used to play football with my friends in the neighbourhood. I'm also a keen follower of football, and often watch the Premier League and Bundesliga matches. Unfortunately, there were few opportunities to play the sport inside the school, and I grew to be unfamiliar with it.

And when I got into college, I found plenty of opportunities to play football, and I have started playing it again. I'm not a professional player, but I love the sport and enjoy playing it with my friends. And now my friends and I often enjoy our time playing football, watching football matches and playing FIFA games together.

Among all kinds of music, my favourites are pop music and R&B.

And Adele, Taylor Swift and Ed Sheeran are my favourite singers.
